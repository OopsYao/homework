    \problem
    Denote $X_t:=W_t^2$, then by Doob's submartingale inequality
    we have that
    \[P\left(\max_{0\leq s\leq t}X_s\leq\lambda\right)
    \leq\frac{1}{\lambda}E(X_t^+)=\frac{t}{\lambda}\]
    as $X_t\geq 0$ implies $E(X_t^+)=E(X_t)=t$.
